Goals

  • Boost Awareness
    Introduce the limited-edition flavors across key channels, achieving a 25% increase in brand visibility by August 31, 2025.
  • Drive Engagement
    Grow Instagram engagement by 30% by encouraging UGC with hashtags like #SummerOfSniffs.
  • Increase Traffic
    Drive 15% more traffic to the Whisker & Tails website and blog by promoting summer-themed content.
  • Grow Product Trials
    Increase sales of seasonal flavors by 20% during the campaign period.

Target Audience

The campaign targets pet parents who see their pets as family and prioritize high-quality nutrition.

  • Demographics
    Age 25-44, Income $60K+, Urban and suburban pet owners in the US
  • Psychographics
    Value sustainability, quality ingredients, and their pets' health. They enjoy documenting their pets' lives and sharing moments on social media.
  • Motivations & Needs
    Providing their pets with the best care and nutrition. Creating memorable bonding moments. Finding trustworthy products that align with personal ethics (sustainability, vet approval).
  • Pain Points
    Difficulty finding tasty, nutrient-rich pet food. Concerns about artificial ingredients and packaging waste.

Key Messaging

  • Primary Message
    Celebrate summer with wholesome, limited-edition meals for your furry friend. Treat them to flavors as vibrant as the season!
  • Secondary Message
    Vet-approved, health-focused recipes made from human-grade ingredients for shiny coats, good digestion, and boundless energy.
  • Eco-Friendly Focus
    You can feel good about every purchase‚Äîmade with sustainability in mind, from the food to the packaging.

Channels and Tactics

Social Media
Instagram-focused UGC contest (#SummerOfSniffs): Share photos or videos of your pet enjoying summer for a chance to win a supply of Whisker & Tails treats. Carousel posts highlighting the summer flavors and their benefits. Summer-themed graphics, such as checklists and playful imagery of pets enjoying outdoor adventures.

Website & Blog
Blog post featuring "5 Fun Ways to Treat Your Pet This Summer (Starting with What's in Their Bowl)" to drive SEO traffic and keep readers engaged. Dedicated landing page for the "Summer of Sniffs" campaign to promote flavors and collect email leads.

Email Marketing
Announce the seasonal launch with an engaging campaign email linked to the product page.
