Campaign Brief

Campaign Brief

Overview

Whisker & Tails, a premium pet food brand dedicated to providing wholesome, all-natural, human-grade ingredients, is launching the "Summer of Sniffs" campaign to celebrate summer with limited-edition seasonal flavors. The campaign will promote "Grilled Salmon Picnic" for cats and "BBQ Chicken Feast" for dogs. With eco-friendly packaging and vet-approved recipes, this initiative aligns with the brand's mission of delivering health-focused, joyful moments for pets and their parents.

The campaign's core objective is to bring awareness and excitement to the seasonal launch while cultivating deeper engagement with pet owners through memorable summer experiences and user-generated content.

Campaign Concept

"Summer of Sniffs" is a lighthearted and cheerful marketing campaign designed to inspire pet owners to treat their furry friends to delicious summer-inspired meals and adventures. The concept ties seasonal nostalgia to Whisker & Tails' limited-edition flavors, driving product trial and fostering shareable moments online. Key visuals and messaging will reflect sunny, playful outdoor activities, tying the joy of summer to a pet's happy and healthy lifestyle—all starting with what's in their bowl.

Goals

  • Boost Awareness
    Introduce the limited-edition flavors across key channels, achieving a 25% increase in brand visibility by August 31, 2025.
  • Drive Engagement
    Grow Instagram engagement by 30% by encouraging UGC with hashtags like #SummerOfSniffs.
  • Increase Traffic
    Drive 15% more traffic to the Whisker & Tails website and blog by promoting summer-themed content.
  • Grow Product Trials
    Increase sales of seasonal flavors by 20% during the campaign period.

Target Audience

The campaign targets pet parents who see their pets as family and prioritize high-quality nutrition.

  • Demographics
    Age 25-44, Income $60K+, Urban and suburban pet owners in the US
  • Psychographics
    Value sustainability, quality ingredients, and their pets' health. They enjoy documenting their pets' lives and sharing moments on social media.
  • Motivations & Needs
    Providing their pets with the best care and nutrition. Creating memorable bonding moments. Finding trustworthy products that align with personal ethics (sustainability, vet approval).
  • Pain Points
    Difficulty finding tasty, nutrient-rich pet food. Concerns about artificial ingredients and packaging waste.

Key Messaging

  • Primary Message
    Celebrate summer with wholesome, limited-edition meals for your furry friend. Treat them to flavors as vibrant as the season!
  • Secondary Message
    Vet-approved, health-focused recipes made from human-grade ingredients for shiny coats, good digestion, and boundless energy.
  • Eco-Friendly Focus
    You can feel good about every purchase—made with sustainability in mind, from the food to the packaging.

Channels and Tactics

Social Media
Instagram-focused UGC contest (#SummerOfSniffs): Share photos or videos of your pet enjoying summer for a chance to win a supply of Whisker & Tails treats. Carousel posts highlighting the summer flavors and their benefits. Summer-themed graphics, such as checklists and playful imagery of pets enjoying outdoor adventures.

Website & Blog
Blog post featuring "5 Fun Ways to Treat Your Pet This Summer (Starting with What's in Their Bowl)" to drive SEO traffic and keep readers engaged. Dedicated landing page for the "Summer of Sniffs" campaign to promote flavors and collect email leads.

Email Marketing
Announce the seasonal launch with an engaging campaign email linked to the product page.

Timeline

  • Kickoff: June 15, 2025
    Finalize campaign creative and launch teaser social posts.
  • Launch: June 20, 2025
    Introduce flavors with posts, blog content, and paid ads.
  • Mid-Campaign: July 10, 2025
    Push UGC contest and engage with user content.
  • Wrap-Up: August 31, 2025
    Conclude with a summary post highlighting favorite user stories and campaign impact.

Deliverables

  • Campaign brief (this document)
  • Blog post on summer pet treats and activities
  • Three Instagram post variants:
    • Carousel highlighting summer flavors
    • UGC-style post featuring a dog licking an ice cube made from Whisker & Tails broth
    • "Summer Checklist" graphic
  • Email campaign copy and design

Measurement and Reporting

KPIs: Instagram engagement rate, hashtag performance (#SummerOfSniffs), Website traffic from campaign landing page, Seasonal product sales uplift, UGC submissions

Reporting Schedule: Weekly campaign updates, final results evaluated by September 10, 2025.

By connecting seasonal fun with wholesome, high-quality meals, Whisker & Tails' "Summer of Sniffs" campaign will elevate the brand and turn summer into a time of joy for pets and their humans.

Blog Post

5 Fun Ways to Treat Your Pet This Summer

Summer is here, and your furry friend deserves to join the fun! At Whisker & Tails, we believe happy pets start with real, natural food. Our new seasonal flavors—"Grilled Salmon Picnic" for cats and "BBQ Chicken Feast" for dogs—bring the best of summer straight to their bowl.

1. Upgrade Their Bowl With a Taste of Summer
Treat your pet to our limited-edition meals, made with all-natural, human-grade ingredients. For cats: "Grilled Salmon Picnic" is packed with Omega-3s for a shiny coat. For dogs: "BBQ Chicken Feast" delivers smoky, savory goodness for summer adventures.

2. Plan a Pet-Friendly Picnic
Take mealtime outside! Pack a picnic with toys, a blanket, and their Whisker & Tails meal. Add vet-approved snacks like sliced cucumbers or frozen blueberries for a cool treat.

3. Beat the Heat With Frozen Treats
Help your pet cool off with icy snacks: freeze "BBQ Chicken Feast" broth for dogs or "Grilled Salmon Picnic" with puréed pumpkin for cats. Supervise while they enjoy!

4. Make a Splash With Water Fun
Many dogs love to cool off in the water. A small, durable kiddie pool can provide hours of fun in your backyard. For a simpler option, a run through the sprinklers can be just as exciting. Even cats can join in—try placing a few ice cubes in their water bowl for them to bat around.

5. Introduce a New Summer Toy
Keep your pet mentally stimulated with a new toy. Look for durable chew toys that can be frozen for extra cooling relief for your dog. For cats, a new wand toy with colorful feathers can provide great indoor exercise and mimic a playful hunt.

Summer's all about joyful memories, and your pet deserves to be part of it. Treat them to Whisker & Tails' all-natural summer flavors—only here for a limited time. A healthy, happy pet is the best summer companion! 🌞🐾
